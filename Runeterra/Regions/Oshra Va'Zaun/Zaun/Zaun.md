Zaun is a polluted undercity to the south of [[Piltover]] Stifled inventors often find their unorthodox research welcomed in Zaun, but reckless industry has rendered whole swathes of the city increasingly toxic. Even so, thanks to a thriving [[Zaun's Criminal Network|black market]] and mechanical augmentation, the people still find ways to prosper.
