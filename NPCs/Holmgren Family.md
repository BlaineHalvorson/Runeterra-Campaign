

## Notable Members
- Harold Holmgren — Patriarch/House Head
	Influence: Low–moderate
	Vibe: Reserved yet constantly concerned about his family losing power
	Appearance: Elderly man, balding white hair, uses a cane to get around and is only able to do so thanks to high tech prosthetics on his legs.
	**Speech:** quiet, man of few words, slow, methodical, and careful to not say anything inappropriate. Wise when he does speak.

- Gladis Holmgren — deceased wife of Harold Holmgren 

- Gwen Holmgren — young daughter of Harold and Gladis
	Influence: none-very low
	Vibe: a literal child
	Appearance: 8 year old girl, always well dressed and socially conscious for her age. Doesn’t play or act like other children her age.
	**Speech:** quiet, very talkative if you can get her started.
