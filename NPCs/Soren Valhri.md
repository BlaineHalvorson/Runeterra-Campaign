Soren Valhri is an [[Shurima (Nation)|Shuriman]] entrepreneur who moved to [[Oshra Va'Zaun]] in 769 AN. Upon moving here he founded a construction company, [[Sandstone & Steel]], which quickly grew to be a prominent [[Merchant clans|merchant clan]] in [[Piltover]], garnering public support as well as increased political power after many large budget contracts ended successfully and brought great wealth to the region.
